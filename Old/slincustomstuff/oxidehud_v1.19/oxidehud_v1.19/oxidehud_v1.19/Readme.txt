------------------------------------------------------------------------------
                                Instructions
------------------------------------------------------------------------------

 - Delete both the \resources and \scripts folders from your steamapps\<account>\Team Fortress 2\tf folder before extracting the HUD.
 - Extract the contents of the appropriate folder into your steamapps\<account>\Team Fortress 2\tf folder.
   - Use 4x3 for resolutions of 800x600, 1024x768, 1152x864, 1280x960 and 1600x1200.
   - Use 5x4 for resolutions of 1280x1024.
   - Use 16x9 for resolutions of 1280x720, 1360x768, 1600x900 and 1920x1080.
   - Use 16x10 for resolutions of 1280x800, 1440x900, 1680x1050 and 1920x1200.
 - Enjoy :)

 - Note: To use the alternative TimePanel or Health Value copy the .res file into your resource\ui folder and overwrite the existing ones.
